Weitai Kang

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student with Prof. Yan Yan in Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) . My research focuses on multimodal learning, particularly for Multimodal Large Language Models and 2D & 3D Visual Grounding. In the meantime, I am a visiting student at University of Central Florida under the supervision of Prof. Mubarak Shah working on 3D large language models. Before that, I earned my bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University in 2022.
